About Me

With a Master's degree in Computer Science, I’ve built a strong foundation in manual and automated testing, requirement analysis, and bug lifecycle management. I’m passionate about delivering reliable and user-centric software through structured testing practices, continuous learning, and close collaboration with cross-functional teams.
